Meet the Avengers

Captain America

Steve Rogers, the first Avenger, stands for justice and freedom.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, the genius, billionaire, and the man behind the iron suit.

Black Panther

T'Challa, the King of Wakanda, fights to protect his kingdom.

Hulk

Bruce Banner, the brilliant scientist with a powerful alter ego.

Wanda Maximoff

Wanda, the Scarlet Witch, wields immense magical powers.

Vision

Vision, created by Stark and Banner, holds the Mind Stone.

Spiderman

Peter Parker, the friendly neighborhood Spiderman, uses his web-slinging powers to fight crime.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, the Black Widow, is a skilled assassin and a core member of the Avengers.

Loki

The God of Mischief, Loki is Thor's brother and often finds himself at odds with the Avengers.

Winter Soldier

Bucky Barnes, once Captain America's best friend, becomes the Winter Soldier, a brainwashed assassin.
